Explanations

why change your filenames?

Often times you save files from many different places, each with their own way of naming files, and it can be nice to have a single naming scheme for files on your computer.

The nice thing about using unix time is being able to see when the file was saved at a glance. Unix time is the number of seconds since january 1st 1970, a sort of arbitrary date to make sure that all unix timestamps will be positive numbers.
